The Importance of Typography in Instructional Design

Typography plays a vital role in how learners engage with instructional content. The choice of font, font size, line spacing, and text alignment all impact readability and comprehension. For example, sans-serif fonts like Arial and Helvetica are often preferred for online reading, as they offer better clarity on digital screens.

It's important to maintain consistency in typography throughout an instructional design project to ensure that learners are not distracted by changes in font or style. Additionally, using the right amount of white space between lines and paragraphs can significantly improve readability.
